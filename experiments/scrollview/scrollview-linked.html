Linked Scrollviews

To attach moving of one scrollview element to another, give the slave scrollview unique ID and add data-linked-x="ID_of_slave_scrollview" (horizontal) or data-linked-y="ID" (for vertical direction) to master scrollview. When the master element is scrolled, the subordinates follow it's direction. Useful for tables with always visible column and row headers like calendar events or accounting reports.
